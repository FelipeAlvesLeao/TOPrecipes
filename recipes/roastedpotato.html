Roasted Potato

  • Back to the main page

    • Roasted potatoes! A recipe that combinates a crunchy and tasty exterior with a soft inside. And what's better is that anyone can do it!


    Ingredients

    • Four medium-sized potatoes
    • Salt and pepper
    • Fresh or Dry herbs to your likening
    • Two cloves of garlic
    • Paprika

    Steps

    1. Cut the potatoes in halves and them halve them one more time. You can do it one more time if you like smaller-sized bites, and you can also peel them if you don't like potato skin.
    2. In a bowl, salt the potatoes and leave them for 5 minutes. Remove the water that is gonna be extracted from them.
    3. Mix in the pepper and the paprika
    4. Start pre-heating your oven to 375ÂºF
    5. Put your potatoes in the baking and place the herbs.
    6. The potatoes must stay at least 20 minutes in the oven.
    7. Enjoy!
