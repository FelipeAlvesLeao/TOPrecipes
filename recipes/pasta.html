Pasta with vegetables

  • Back to the main page

    • Pasta is one of the most commonplace foods, and they're delicious and easy to cook with some fried vegetables!


    Ingredients

    • Broccoli
    • Grated carrots
    • Any pasta of your liking. We personally like fusilli with this recipe.
    • Chopped onions
    • Oregano
    • Any extra vegetables that you would like
    • Chopped garlic

    Steps

    1. Bring water to a boil. For every 100g of pasta, you should use one liter of water.
    2. Use the instructions on the package as a guide of how much time the pasta should stay in the hot water
    3. Drain the pasta
    4. Heat olive oil in a stir-fry pan
    5. Add garlic and onion to your pan
    6. Add broccoli and any other vegetables you choose.Add the ones you'd like to eat in a more raw fashion last
    7. Add the grated carrots and the pasta, fry them for a bit until mixed
    8. Enjoy!
