Brazilian Pancake

  • Back to the main page

    • In Brazil, what people call pancakes might confuse you - they are nothing alike the pancakes you might be used to. This dish is an easy-to-make and delicious meal that you can prepare for your whole family, consisting of a very thin and light dough and ground meat with tomato sauce filling, with cheese on the top.


    Ingredients

    Dough
    • One and a half cups of wheat flour
    • One cup of milk
    • Two eggs
    • 4 tablespoons of vegetable oil
    Filling
    • 300g of ground meat
    • 2 tablespoons of chopped onions
    • Half tomato cut in cubes
    • Half a can of crushed tomatoes
    • 400g of any grated cheese

    Steps

      Dough
    1. Put the eggs, milk and the vegetable oil in the blender. Put the wheat flour in small amounts until all of it is blended.
    2. After blending, add a pinch of salt.
    3. Add a very small amount of vegetable oil to a nonstick pan
    4. Spill enough of your dough to cover the bottom of the pan and make circular motions so that it becomes round.
    5. When the dough is not liquid anymore, flip it so that it toasts a little on the other side too
    6. Repeat until you run out of dough.
      Filling
      7. Saut√© your onions until they're golden.
    7. Add your meat and season it. Let it cook until water is coming out of it.
    8. Add your tomatoes and crushed tomatoes and also season the sauce.
    9. Cook for 10 minutes or until your sauce is thicker
    10. Put the filling in your fried dough and roll them
    11. Put your brazilian pancakes in a sheet, cover them with a bit of your remaining sauce and then cover with grated cheese
      12. Enjoy!
